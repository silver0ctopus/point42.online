# Manifesto

# 📜 The Manifesto of Point 42

While anyone may visit Point 42, the status of Signat is reserved exclusively for those who uphold the provisions of this Manifesto and demonstrate a readiness to contribute to the development of the community.

Every Signat possesses the right to respect, the right to the inviolability of their personal space, and the right to express and exercise their will, provided it does not infringe upon the rights of other Signats. This territory is declared free from any ideologies that restrict the rights of its Signats.

This Manifesto stands as the supreme law of Point 42 and the foundation for its entire legal framework.

The Vertex — a non-elected monarch — serves as the Guarantor of the inviolability of this Manifesto, as well as the head of the executive and judicial branches. Legislative power is exercised by the Supreme Council. Every Signat holds the right to be elected to the Supreme Council.

Thus, by this Manifesto, a governance of the Signats, by the Signats, and for the Signats is established within the territory of Point 42.
